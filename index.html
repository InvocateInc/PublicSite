your data, your rules

Most of the world's data never moves. The value of sharing it is real, but the risk of exposing it is greater, so data stay locked in organizational silos with one costly consequence: the collaborations, models, and discoveries they could power never happen. invocate's Escrow Agent changes the equation: when any two organizations can compute on each other's data and provably receive only an agreed result - nothing leaked, nothing left behind - the value of data can move even when the data cannot.
